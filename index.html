BTC / BTC5M Weekly Liquidity Rhythm

A shareable operating dashboard for deciding when BTC5M bots are swimming with liquidity vs. being exposed to thin overnight/weekend microstructure. Cells are weekday × UTC hour. Binance metrics are averaged over the last 60 days; Polymarket BTC5M cells come from Studio1 local CLOB tape last_trade_price events.

Read this correctly: high BTC futures volume/open interest means the global BTC market is active; it does not prove Polymarket BTC5M edge. Thin hours are risk flags for stale quotes, whale impact, and adverse selection. Options history is not in this cut; futures/open-interest/taker flow are used as derivatives proxies.

    Operational takeaways

    How to use this for bots

    • Prefer evaluation/trading windows where futures volume, trade count, and Polymarket BTC5M trade events are simultaneously high.
    • Treat low-volume UTC night/weekend cells as adverse-selection zones: widen gates, reduce size, or disable maker quotes.
    • Compare weekday vs weekend: if weekend derivatives activity is low and range is high, avoid strategies trained on weekday flow.
    • For BTC5M specifically, each UTC hour contains 12 market resolutions. markets_touched approximates how many 5m windows had local observed trades.
